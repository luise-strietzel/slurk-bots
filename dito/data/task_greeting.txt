**Welcome to the DiTo Game!**


For this game you need to provide decriptions and discuss with your partner. Remember the following command to play the game:

(1) Type **/same** or **/different** once you and your partner think you are done.
(2) If you agree that your views are different, provide a brief description of the change together with the **/different** command.
For example : **/different no red pyramid**


Please type **/ready** to begin the game.
